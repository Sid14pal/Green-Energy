Customer Management

How can add vendor role to the customer?

To give a customer the vendor role, log in to the admin panel of the store. Go to the User Management / Customers section, select the customer’s profile, and update their role to Vendor. Once updated, the customer will gain access to vendor features such as adding products, managing inventory, and tracking orders.

What benefits a customer can take?

Customers can enjoy exclusive discounts, loyalty rewards, seasonal offers, and free delivery on selected orders. By creating an account, you can also track your orders and save your favorite products for easy reordering.

How to change customer's address?

Simply log in to your account, go to My Profile > Address Book, and update your shipping or billing address. Make sure to save changes before placing your next order.

Product Type

What is difference between simple & variable product?

A simple product is a single item with no variations (e.g., 1kg of organic rice). A variable product allows you to choose options like size, weight, or flavor (e.g., 250g, 500g, or 1kg of organic honey).

How to buy several products at once?

Add all your desired products to the cart, then proceed to checkout. You can adjust quantities or remove items before confirming your order.

How to add downloadable product?

Downloadable items like organic recipe guides or wellness e-books can be purchased like normal products. After payment, a secure download link will be available in your account and email

The Others

How can I return an purchased item online?

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, go to My Orders > Return Request, fill out the form, and our support team will arrange pickup or replacement within 3–5 business days.

How to add coupon code?

During checkout, enter your coupon code in the “Apply Coupon” box. The discount will be applied instantly to your order total.

How can I find theme style?

Our store follows a natural, eco-friendly, and wellness-driven theme. You can browse by categories like “Organic Foods,” “Personal Care,” or “Eco Essentials” to find what fits your lifestyle.

What are the delivery types you can use?

We provide standard delivery (3–5 days), express delivery (1–2 days), and same-day delivery (in select cities). Shipping is free above a certain order value.
